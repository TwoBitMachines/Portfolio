Broforce — Micro Project

▼
1) Example of chain reaction 2) AI graph 3) Barrel graph

A Broforce inspired micro-project built to explore reaction-based AI systems. The goal was to create a system of cascading interactions involving environmental damage and agent responses. The AI graphs demanded careful architectural structure. I used my node editor, Flow Grid, to keep all behaviour logic organized and manageable.


  • Chain Reactions: A single bullet can trigger an entire cascade. It hits a barrel, which explodes and ignites the fuse timers of nearby barrels. Fire spreads across tiles based on probability, burning through structures and causing collapse. Barrels without support fall and detonate on impact. Enemies caught in the blast may survive but catch fire, causing them to panic and run back and forth, sometimes falling off ledges. They normally patrol and enter an alert state when the player is nearby. The whole level behaves as one system, with destruction and fire cascading naturally through the environment.
  • Cross-Object Communication: The core challenge was managing communication between objects. Barrels, tiles, enemies, and the player could all trigger one another through reaction chains, which quickly became too complex for traditional script references. To solve this, each object broadcasts and receives event signals through its node graph, allowing reactions to cascade cleanly across the entire level without hard-coded links. This architecture kept the system organized and manageable.
